About Kistie_Store

Kistie_Store is a Kampala retail fashion business built around carefully selected clothing brands from Turkey, the UK, and the USA, with worldwide shipping for customers who shop from anywhere.

The Ethos

Fashionably Sorted means every piece should feel intentional. The store focuses on polished imported fashion, wearable styling, and a shopping experience that feels personal from first browse to final purchase message.

Vision

To become a trusted Kampala destination for imported women’s fashion, known for reliable sizing, premium presentation, and easy WhatsApp-led ordering.

Mission

Offer curated clothing brands from Turkey, the UK, and the USA while helping customers shop confidently with EU sizing, direct WhatsApp purchase support, and worldwide shipping from Kampala, Uganda.
